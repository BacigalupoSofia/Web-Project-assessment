Welcome to Vina del Mar Fencing Club!

Where discipline and a passion for combat come together to create great athletes and even better people.

Here we foster respect, technique, and sportsmanship in every training session, offering an inclusive environment for both beginners and advanced competitors.

We invite you to discover the excitement of fencing !

Esgrima Vina (EEVM) is a fencing school in Vina del Mar that works with kids, teenagers and adults in the Olympic epee modality.

We are affiliated with the Chilean Fencing Federation, so you can train with a technical, physical and tactical focus, for those who just want to learn as well as for those who want to compete.
